  • Stay hydrated and rest well
  • Apply calamine lotion for itch relief
  • Avoid scratching to prevent scars (khujli nhi krna ka mtlan nhi krne ka)
  • Wear loose, comfortable cotton clothing
  • Isolate until all blisters scab over
  • Consult a doctor if fever is high or symptoms worsen
